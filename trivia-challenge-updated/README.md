# Trivia Challenge

Trivia Challenge is a web-based quiz game where users answer questions from different years and topics. It features sound effects, a countdown timer, keyboard controls, and a clean mobile-friendly UI.

## Features
- Multiple topics and years of trivia
- Timer with sound and visual warnings
- Keyboard (A/B/C/D) and touch/click support
- Score tracking and high score saving
- Mute toggle and background music
- Confetti and emoji celebration

## Current Trivia Stats
- Total Questions: 6
- Topics: Science, Pop Culture, Technology, History

## How to Play
1. Select a year and topic
2. Answer each question before time runs out
3. View your score and try to beat your high score!
